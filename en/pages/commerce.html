Delivery Time (Service Provision Period)

Product Delivery Time / Service Start Time
Varies depending on the service. Details will be provided separately at the time of contract. Generally, after the contract is finalized, we anticipate a period of one week to several months, depending on the scale of the project, which will be decided in consultation with the customer.

Policy on Exchanges and Returns

Returns/Exchanges from Customers (for non-defective products)
As our software development services are custom-made based on customer requirements, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges. However, if the delivered product differs from the contract, we will make corrections if notified within 7 days of receipt. Details will be specified in the individual contract.
Returns/Exchanges for Defective Products/Services
If there are clear defects or the delivered product differs from the contract, please contact customer support (me@lumina-group.jp) within 7 days of receipt, and we will make corrections. The allocation of costs in such cases will be specified in the individual contract.
