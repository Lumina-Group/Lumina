About Lumina Development

Lumina Developmentについて

Lumina Development was founded in 2025 by a team dedicated to developing quantum-resistant security solutions that are accessible to all, in anticipation of the coming era of quantum computing.

Currently, development is led by a single founder, but we are actively looking for engineers and researchers who share our vision to build a more secure future together.

We welcome individuals from diverse backgrounds to collaborate in transforming cutting-edge cryptography into practical security solutions.
