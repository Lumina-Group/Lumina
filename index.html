About Lumina Development

Lumina Developmentについて

Founded in 2025, Lumina Development is dedicated to creating quantum-resistant security solutions that are accessible to everyone, in anticipation of the quantum computing era.

Currently led by a passionate founder, we are actively seeking engineers and researchers who share our vision to build a more secure future together.

We welcome talented individuals from diverse backgrounds to join our mission of transforming cutting-edge cryptography into practical security solutions for tomorrow's digital world.

2025年に設立されたLumina Developmentは、量子コンピューティング時代を見据え、誰もが利用できる量子耐性セキュリティソリューションの開発に取り組んでいます。

現在は創業者が主導していますが、より安全な未来を共に築く志を持つエンジニアや研究者を積極的に募集しています。
